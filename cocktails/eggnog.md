## Eggnog

#### Ingredients

* 6 eggs
* 1 1/2 cup cream of coconut
* 1 qt milk
* 2 cup heavy cream
* 1 Tbl vanilla
* 1 cup dark rum
* ground nutmeg

#### Directions

1. In a large bowl, beat eggs until fluffy.
2. Beat in cream of coconut.
3. Gradually beat in milk and cream.
4. Stir in vanilla.
5. Chill for several hours.
6. Serve with ground nutmeg on top.
